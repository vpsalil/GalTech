My Favourite foods

  2. Rice and Fish Curry
  3. Rice and Manga Pulisseri

The softness of puttu with the spicy, slow‑cooked beef makes every bite unforgettable. I also love Rice and Fish Curry, a meal that carries the true taste of Kerala.
